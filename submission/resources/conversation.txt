Interview Summary — Amara Wanjiku Photography
Conducted by: Christian Wainaina
Date: June 2026

---

I interviewed Amara Wanjiku, a freelance photographer based in Nairobi, Kenya,
who has been working professionally for over eight years. She specialises in
portrait, wedding, wildlife, and street photography.

Key points from our conversation:

- WHO: Amara is a self-employed photographer serving individuals, couples,
  corporates, and media organisations across Kenya.

- TARGET AUDIENCE: Potential clients looking for portrait, wedding, or event
  photography services. Secondary audience: international travel and wildlife
  media buyers.

- MOST IMPORTANT INFORMATION: Her portfolio/gallery, the services she offers
  with pricing starting points, and her contact details so clients can book
  a session directly.

- VISUAL STYLE: Amara preferred a dark, elegant aesthetic — she described it
  as "editorial and timeless." She liked gold/amber accent colours against
  a dark background. She wanted large, full-bleed images to let the
  photography speak for itself.

- DESIRED USER ACTION: Visitors should be able to browse her work, understand
  what services are available, and then reach her via the contact form or
  by phone/email to book a session.

- EXTRA PREFERENCES: She wanted the site to work well on mobile since most
  of her client referrals come through Instagram and WhatsApp. She did NOT
  want heavy text — short, punchy copy only.
